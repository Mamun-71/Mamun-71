### 🌟 Featured Projects

#### 🇧🇩 [NISE — National Employment Platform](https://nise.gov.bd/)
National Intelligence for Skills, Education, Employment & Entrepreneurship — a government platform serving **500,000+ users**. Contributed to microservices-based backend architecture and led the end-to-end migration from the legacy iSEC platform.
> `Laravel` `MySQL` `Microservices` `React` `Kafka`

#### ⚖️ [BDLaws — Legal Information Platform](https://bdlaws.smart-ai.dev/en)
Comprehensive Bangladesh legal information platform with full-text legal search, Bengali/English internationalization, an admin backoffice, and the BDCode legal-code viewer.
> `Next.js 15` `NestJS` `MongoDB` `TypeScript` `Tailwind CSS`

#### 🎓 [GTMS — Graduate Tracing Management System](https://gtms.btebcbt.gov.bd/)
LinkedIn-style alumni networking and career-tracking platform for the **Bangladesh Technical Education Board**. Designed and developed RESTful APIs for graduate profile management, job placement tracking, and role-based access control.
> `Next.js 15` `Laravel` `MySQL` `TypeScript` `Tailwind CSS`

#### 🏛️ [Dak Bungalow & Auditorium Booking System](https://kzpdb-booking.com/)
Full-featured online reservation platform for **Kishoreganj Zilla Parishad**. Processed **BDT 1.5M+** in secure digital transactions with reconciliation and validation mechanisms.
> `Laravel` `MySQL` `Blade` `Bootstrap`

#### 🏘️ Municipality Management System
Multi-tenant municipality management system handling trade licenses, holding tax, and utility bill payments (water, electricity, gas), with PayStation gateway integration.
> `NestJS` `Next.js` `MySQL` `Multi-tenant Architecture`
